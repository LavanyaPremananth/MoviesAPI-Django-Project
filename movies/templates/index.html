Welcome to the Movies App!

Explore the fantastic world of movies with our RESTful API. Below are the key endpoints to get you started.

API Description API Format Example
Retrieves a paginated list of all movies available in the database. movies Explore Movies
Creates a new entry in the movie database. movies Add a Movie
Retrieves detailed information about a specific movie identified by its unique ID. movies/{id} View Movie Details
Updates the details of an existing movie identified by its unique ID. movies/{id} Edit Movie
Partially updates the details of an existing movie identified by its unique ID. movies/{id} Modify Movie
Deletes the entry of a movie identified by its unique ID. movies/{id} Remove Movie
